About Us

Who we are

Trinity Pharmacare Limited, founded in 2023, is a reputable pharmaceutical company headquartered in the vibrant city of Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the name of your pharmaceutical brand, and what products do you offer?

Our brand name is Trinity Pharmacare LTD, and we offer a wide range of pharmaceutical products including over-the-counter medications, prescription drugs, and supplements.

Are your pharmaceutical products approved by the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC)?

Yes, all our products are rigorously evaluated and approved by NAFDAC to ensure their safety, efficacy, and quality.

Where can I purchase your products in Nigeria? Do you have a list of authorized distributors or pharmacies?

You can purchase our products from licensed pharmacies, hospitals, and healthcare facilities across Nigeria. We have a list of authorized distributors available on our website.

What are the active ingredients in your medications, and how do they work?

Each of our medications contains specific active ingredients carefully selected to address particular health conditions. We provide detailed information about active ingredients and their mechanisms of action on the product packaging and inserts.

Do you offer over-the-counter (OTC) medications, prescription drugs, or both?

We offer both over-the-counter medications for common ailments and prescription drugs for more serious health conditions. Our products cater to diverse healthcare needs.

What conditions or ailments do your products treat or manage?

Our products cover a wide range of health conditions including but not limited to pain relief, cough and cold, allergies, gastrointestinal disorders, cardiovascular health, and more. Detailed information about indications is available on product labels.

Are there any potential side effects associated with your pharmaceutical products?

Like all medications, our products may have potential side effects. We provide comprehensive information about possible side effects, contraindications, and precautions on product labels and inserts. It's important to follow the recommended dosage and consult a healthcare professional if you experience any adverse reactions.

How should I store your medications properly to maintain their efficacy?

Our medications should be stored according to the instructions provided on the packaging, typically in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Proper storage helps maintain their potency and effectiveness.

Do you offer any patient assistance programs or discounts for your products?

Yes, we have various patient assistance programs and discount schemes available for eligible customers. Please contact our customer service or visit our website for more information on available programs.

Can I take your medications with other prescription drugs or supplements?

It's important to consult with a healthcare professional before taking any new medication, especially if you are already taking prescription drugs or supplements. They can advise on potential interactions and ensure your safety.
