About Us

Our Story

Trinity Pharmacare Limited, founded on January 29th, 2023, is a reputable pharmaceutical company headquartered in the vibrant city of Ibadan, Oyo State. Conveniently located in Pacesetter Bitek Estate, just after the DSTV Office in Onireke, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry.

With a primary focus on wholesale, distribution, and marketing of pharmaceutical products and medical supplies, we are committed to providing essential healthcare solutions to our community and beyond.

At Trinity Pharmacare, we believe in upholding the highest standards of integrity, ensuring that honesty, transparency, and fairness are at the core of all our operations. Our dedication to quality drives us to continuously innovate and improve, guaranteeing that our products and services meet the stringent requirements of the healthcare industry.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. We are deeply committed to understanding and exceeding the needs of our customers, building long-lasting relationships based on trust and reliability.

Driven by a passion for excellence, we are constantly striving to set new benchmarks in the industry, delivering outstanding results in everything we do. At Trinity Pharmacare Limited, we are more than just a pharmaceutical company; we are your partners in health, dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of those we serve.

Our Mission

Mission Statement

To partner with health stakeholders across the nation with the aim of improving the wellbeing and health of people by offering access to needed drug products to all our customers.

We are dedicated to ensuring that everyone has access to high-quality, affordable medications, regardless of their location or socioeconomic status.

Through our partnerships and distribution network, we strive to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and patients, ensuring that essential medications are readily available when needed the most.

Our Vision

Vison Statement

To become a leading provider of pharmaceutical products and medical supplies, known for our commitment to integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction, while making a positive impact on the health and wellbeing of our community.

We envision a future where everyone has access to the medications they need to live healthier, happier lives, and we are dedicated to making this vision a reality through our unwavering dedication and hard work.

By fostering strong partnerships with healthcare stakeholders, we aim to create a healthier nation where no one is left behind in their pursuit of good health.
